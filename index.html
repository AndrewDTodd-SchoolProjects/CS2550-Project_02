My personal interests

I am a game developer. I am interested in pretty much all aspects of the game development process. I am particularly interested/involved in game systems engineering. I also have an interest in 3D Modeling though.

I also enjoy telling stories. This overlaps with my love of game development, as I believe it is the best medium for storytelling. Other than games though I am also a writer. I would like to extend some of my book narratives into the realm of video games.

When I am not involved in creating worlds either in text or on the screen I like to enjoy the ones others have built. I have a love for old movies and tv (like the golden age of hollywood). I think the vast majority of the excessive quantity of media coming out today is either terrible, or is just not really good enough to consume even though it may not be totally trash.
